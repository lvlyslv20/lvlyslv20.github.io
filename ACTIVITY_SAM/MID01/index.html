PROFILE

The Minions are small antagonists, yellow, capsule shaped creatures, all-male, who have one or two eyes that were created by Eric Guillon. They bring much of the comedy in the film, and they are known as the stealer of the movie. They frequently speak in an incomprehensible language, called Minionese, occasionally switching to English.

TRIVIA!

1. Minions are the stars of the mobile games Minion Rush and Minions Paradise.

2. The Minions take their name from the company name of IlluMination.

3. Other than bananas, the Minions really love butts, they won't hesitate to put their butts on a copier machine.

4. Otto is currently the only minion with braces.

5. Minions have only three fingers, possibly because most classic cartoon characters are drawn with only three (or four or five) fingers.
